# Gaps & Challenges Observed

[← Market Usage](ai-usage-patterns.md) | [Next: Problem Statement](problem-statement.md)

Across scenarios, recurring gaps emerge.

## 1. Lack of Persistent Structure

Decisions are re-inferred each time.

## 2. Weak Coordination

Multi-file and multi-step changes are manually managed.

## 3. Implicit Decisions

Choices are made without explicit visibility or control.

## 4. Limited Traceability

Intent → implementation → validation are not clearly linked.

## 5. Manual Consistency Enforcement

Developers ensure alignment themselves.

## Cross-Cutting Insight

```text
AI assists execution, but structure and coherence remain external to the tools.
```
